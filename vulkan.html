Vulkan Android Libs v0.2.0

Unlock high-performance graphics on Android! Lightweight Vulkan library giving full GPU access for smooth, fast, and visually stunning apps. Ideal for games, emulators, AR/VR apps, and custom rendering engines. Compatible with Sodium, Fabric, and other mods.

Download Vulkan Libs

- Android 8.0+
- Minimum 4 GB RAM (8 GB+ recommended)
- Java/Kotlin project setup

1. Download vulkanMod-android-libs-0.2.0.jar.
2. Move it to your mods libs folder.
3. Paste in mods folder:

dependencies {
    needed files Vulkan(of your Minecraft Version)
}
4. Start Minecraft And Enjoy!.
